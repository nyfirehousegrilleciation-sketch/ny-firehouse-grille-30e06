Frequently Asked Questions About Private Events

How far in advance should I book a private event at NY Firehouse Grille in Peekskill?
We recommend booking your private event at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially for weekend dates or during peak seasons like graduation and holiday periods in Peekskill. This gives us enough time to customize your menu, secure your preferred date and space, and discuss any special requests. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute bookings when possible, so feel free to reach out even if your event is coming up soon.
What is the pricing structure for hosting a private event, and are there any minimums or fees?
Our pricing for private events is based on the space reserved, the size of your party, and your menu selections. We offer both buffet and plated options, as well as bar packages. For most private events, we have a food and beverage minimum that varies depending on the day and time—typically starting around $1,000 for smaller gatherings. There’s no separate room rental fee for most events, but a deposit is required to secure your date. We’re happy to provide a customized quote based on your specific needs.
Can NY Firehouse Grille provide a full bar for private events, and is your venue licensed for alcohol service?
Yes, NY Firehouse Grille is fully licensed to serve beer, wine, and cocktails at our Peekskill location. We offer customizable bar packages ranging from cash bars to open bars, and can accommodate special requests for signature drinks. All alcohol service is provided by our trained staff in compliance with New York State regulations, and guests must be 21 or older to be served alcoholic beverages.
What is the process for planning a private event at NY Firehouse Grille?
Once you reach out to us, our event coordinator will schedule a call or in-person meeting to discuss your vision, guest count, menu preferences, and any special accommodations. We’ll walk you through our menu options, bar packages, and available spaces (including our upstairs room and outdoor patio, weather permitting). After finalizing details, we’ll send you a contract and request a deposit to secure your booking. We’ll stay in touch to finalize your headcount and any last-minute details as your event date approaches.
Do you guarantee exclusive use of your space for private events, and what happens if something goes wrong on the day?
Depending on your group size and the area reserved, we can guarantee exclusive use of our upstairs private room or entire venue for larger events. This will be clearly outlined in your contract. Our team is committed to making your event a success—if any issues arise (such as weather impacting outdoor events or last-minute changes), we’ll work closely with you to provide solutions, including moving indoors or adjusting the setup as needed. Your event coordinator will be on site the day of your event to ensure everything runs smoothly.
